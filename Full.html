Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?
The timeline depends on the complexity of your project. A basic website typically takes 1-2 weeks, while more complex e-commerce or custom solutions can take 4-8 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase.
Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?
Yes! We offer comprehensive support packages ranging from 3 months to 12 months depending on your plan. This includes security updates, content updates, technical support, and performance monitoring.
Can you help with digital marketing and SEO?
Absolutely! We offer complete digital marketing services including SEO optimization, social media marketing, Google Ads management, and lead generation campaigns. All our websites come with basic SEO optimization.
What's included in the website development cost?
Our pricing includes responsive design, mobile optimization, basic SEO, contact forms, Google Analytics setup, and the specified support period. Additional features like e-commerce, advanced integrations, or custom functionality may have additional costs.
Do you work with businesses outside Delhi?
While we're based in Delhi, we work with clients across India and internationally. We conduct meetings via video calls and use modern collaboration tools to ensure seamless project delivery regardless of location.
Can I update the website content myself?
Yes! We build websites with user-friendly content management systems that allow you to easily update text, images, and other content. We also provide training and documentation to help you manage your website independently.
